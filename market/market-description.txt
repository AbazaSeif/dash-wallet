Have your Darkcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Darkcoin Wallet is the first mobile Darkcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Darkcoin amount in DRK, mDRK and µDRK.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Darkcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Darkcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Darkcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Darkcoin balance.

You can find more about Darkcoin at
http://darkcoin.io

If you want to contribute to Darkcoin Wallet, the project is located at
